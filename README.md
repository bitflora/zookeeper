# The Ultimate Zookeeper
https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/the-ultimate-zookeeper

A Minecraft challenge pack oriented around gathering animals.

Main design goals:
- Include as many animals as possible, orient the pack around collecting them for your zoo.
- All automation should be performed by the animals; no Create or similar tech mods.
- There should be multiple ways to complete quests; different animals should work, though they may involve different trade-offs.
- It always pays to collect another animal; as your zoo expands, the emeralds you get from zoogoers will also increase.

Chapter 1: Start building a basic farm with the Genetic Animals versions of vanilla farm animals. 
Chapter 2: Tame Alex's Rats, who provide most of the core automation mechanics.
Chapter 3: Build a ~~smeltery~~ sciencery to ~~melt~~ science certain laboratory animals in.
Chapter 4: Bring back dinosaurs.
Comclusion: Resurrect Steve Irwin.
